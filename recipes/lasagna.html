Lasagna

This is a delicious baked lasagna recipe. This recipe is perfect for thanksgiving.

Ingredients

  • lasagna pasta
  • ground meat of your choice
  • cheese of your choice
  • water

Steps

  1. boil hot water
  2. let pasta sit in boiled water for 20 minutes
  3. cook meat
  4. put hot boiled pasta in tray
  5. layer pasta then meat then cheese then repeat for few layers
  6. put in oven and cook for 45min-1 hour
